About focustripod_vz

In June 2022, a dream took shape in the form of focustripod_vz, a creative venture founded by Marvellers Baloyi. What began as a hobby soon blossomed into a full-fledged business, driven by Marvellers' passion for capturing life's precious moments through photography and videography. It all started with Marvellers taking pictures with his phone, which earned him compliments and encouragement from friends and family. As he continued to hone his skills, he began to see the world in a different light, and his passion for photography and videography grew. He eventually started shooting reels, and the positive feedback he received solidified his decision to turn his hobby into a career. Marvellers' journey was not without its challenges, but he was fortunate to have a strong support system. His girlfriend, Phenyo, played a significant role in helping him grow the business, offering valuable advice and encouragement along the way. Additionally, Nenungwi was a constant source of guidance and support, helping Marvellers navigate the ups and downs of entrepreneurship. Fast forward to 2025, focustripod_vz has served over 50 clients, delivering satisfactory results that have helped build a loyal customer base.
